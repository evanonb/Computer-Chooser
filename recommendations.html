Recommendations

Providing a catalogue of recomended computers for your specific needs

Best Value

Acer Swift 3

Best Value

With an emphasis on minimalism and an incredible focus on what really matters in a laptop, the Acer Swift series is prominant in great value laptops. Expect great performance and an impressive experience for its surprising price!

Best Performance

Razer Blade 15

Best Performance

The best performance laptops are usually gaming laptops, but the Razer Blade 15 stands out among its peers in being a stealthy and thin laptop that makes minimal compromises in performance.

Best Longevity

Apple Macbook Air 2020

Best Longevity

One of the best and well-rounded macbooks, the 2020 model of this beloved laptop features a powerful Apple Silicon M1 processor for lightning fast speed and longevity. With incredible long-time support, expect this device to last for 7+ years.

Best Windows

Dell XPS 13 9310

Best Windows

Dell has a reputation for unbelievably fast and well performing laptops. The XPS 13 is one of their most beloved series and stands out from the competition in its well rounded design that encapsulates its attention to detail and focus on creating the best windows laptop for all users.

Best Mac

Apple Macbook Pro 2021

Best Mac

The Apple Silicon M1 processor in 2020 revolutionized the performance of laptops, but in 2021 Apple released the Macbook Pro with the M1 Pro processor. In addition to the impressive Macbook Pro, this new iteration definitely takes the cake as the best Macbook.

Best Chromebook

Acer Spin 311

Best Chromebook

Acer is a very reputable manufacturer, and their Spin 311 Chromebook is no exception to their impecable quality in laptops. The ChromeOS is incredibly intuitve and perfect for students and first-time computer owners.
